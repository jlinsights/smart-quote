# Saving, sharing, and exporting a quote as PDF

> Audience: forwarding partners · Estimated read time: 2 minutes

Once a quote looks right on screen, you have three options: save it for
yourself, share a read-only link with your customer, or download a
branded PDF. They're not mutually exclusive — most partners do all three.

## Saving a quote

Click **Save Quote** at the top of the result panel. You'll be prompted
to add an optional note — this is useful for remembering context like
"urgent Frankfurt Airport pickup" or "customer confirmed packing type".

Once saved, the quote gets a reference number in the format
`SQ-YYYY-NNNN` — for example, `SQ-2026-0142`. This reference is unique
across the whole platform and is how you (and we) refer to the quote
later.

Saved quotes live under the **History** tab. You can filter by date,
destination, carrier, or status, and search by reference number.

## Sharing a read-only link

From any saved quote's detail view, click **Share Link**. BridgeLogis
generates a unique URL that you can copy to your customer — by email,
WhatsApp, or any channel you like.

The link:

- Shows only the essential information: origin, destination, carrier,
  total, and transit time.
- Does **not** expose your margin, cost breakdown, or internal notes.
- Works without the customer needing to sign in.
- Expires after 7 days by default. If you need a longer window, let us
  know and we'll extend it.

This is the cleanest way to send a quote externally — much better than
screenshotting the calculator.

## Exporting as PDF

From the same detail view, click the PDF icon. The PDF includes:

- Reference number, creation date, and your company name
- Full carrier breakdown (base rate, FSC, add-ons, margin)
- Packing details, billable weight, and zone
- Any notes you added when saving
- A valid-until date (defaults to 7 days from creation)

The PDF is branded with BridgeLogis and formatted for direct forward
to customers. You can also print it or attach it to an email.

## A tip for power users

If you quote the same lane repeatedly, use the **Duplicate** button on
a saved quote. It copies all the inputs to a fresh quote you can tweak
— changing just the weight or packing type while keeping destination,
carrier, and FSC consistent. Much faster than re-entering everything.

## Questions?

If a saved quote looks off or you want us to check something before you
send it to the customer, click the **Ask** button on the detail view.
We'll get the quote reference automatically so there's no back-and-forth.
