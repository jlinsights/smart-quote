# Product Mission (Lite)

Smart Quote System is an international logistics quoting platform for Goodman GLS & J-Ways that instantly calculates shipping costs across UPS, DHL, and EMAX carriers with real-time cost breakdowns, margin analysis, and quote persistence.

The platform serves two audiences: internal operations staff (via /admin) who generate and manage quotes daily, and external customers (via /dashboard) who access self-service quoting with live exchange rates, port weather for 47 global locations, logistics news, and account manager contact info. The calculation engine runs identical logic on frontend (TypeScript) and backend (Ruby) simultaneously, delivering instant UI responsiveness with backend-validated accuracy across 200+ destination countries. Full i18n support for English, Korean, Chinese, and Japanese.
